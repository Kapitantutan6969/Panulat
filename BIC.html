BIC Cristal

Made by the company BIC. this is a disposable, inexpensive, and mass-produced pen. It was the best selling pen in the 1950s.

This is the ultimate student pen, almost everyone has it. The hexagonal structure of the pen helps people how to hold in a three-grip point fashion which makes it very ergonomic.

Costing at around 10-11 peso range, this is absolutely adored by many students.

Penpoints: 9/10
